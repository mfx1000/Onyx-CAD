ZONE C Process

Three steps.
Under two minutes.

01

Upload your STEP file

Drag and drop any STEP file. The geometry processes in seconds, hundreds of faces, automatically fingerprinted and ready to annotate.

02

Click faces, assign specs

Click any surface in the 3D viewer. Set tolerances, thread types, colors. Use the heat map to visualize your tolerance distribution across the whole part.

03

Export or share a link

Export an annotated STEP file with metadata baked in. Or copy a share link, anyone with it can view the annotated model in a browser, no account needed.
