02 — The service

OnyxCAD provides a browser-based tool that allows users to:

— Upload STEP files and view them in a 3D browser viewer.
— Annotate individual faces of a 3D model with tolerances, thread specifications, colours, and notes.
— Export annotated STEP files with metadata embedded in the file structure.
— Generate shareable read-only links to annotated models for viewing by third parties without an account.

The number of active projects available to you at any time depends on your subscription plan.
